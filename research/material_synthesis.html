Material Synthesis using MAPs

Research Overview

Our research explores novel material synthesis routes using Multi-Anvil Presses (MAPs) to create materials with unique properties not achievable through conventional methods.

Key Research Areas:

  • High-pressure polymorph synthesis
  • Nanostructured material fabrication
  • Metastable phase stabilization

Recent Findings

We've successfully synthesized several new high-entropy ceramics with exceptional mechanical and thermal properties under extreme pressure conditions.
