Extreme Conditions in MAPs

Research Overview

This research focuses on studying material behavior under extreme conditions using MAPs (Multi-Anvil Presses). Our work investigates phase transitions, mechanical properties, and chemical reactions at high pressures and temperatures.

Key Research Areas:

  • High-pressure phase transitions
  • Material stability under extreme conditions
  • In-situ characterization techniques

Recent Findings

Our recent work has revealed several novel high-pressure phases in transition metal oxides, with potential applications in energy storage and catalysis.
