Research Overview

Our group develops and applies advanced in-situ characterization techniques for studying materials under extreme conditions in Multi-Anvil Presses (MAPs).

Key Research Areas:

  • High-pressure X-ray diffraction
  • In-situ electrical measurements
  • Synchrotron radiation techniques
